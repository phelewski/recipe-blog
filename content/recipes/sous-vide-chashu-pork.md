## Timing

- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 8 hours
- **Total Time:** 8 hours

## Ingredients

- 3 inches of peeled ginger (chopped)
- 2 green onions (chopped)
- ½ cup soy sauce
- ½ cup mirin
- ¼ cup fish sauce
- ½ cup plain white sugar
- 1 pork belly (skin on or off)

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix together with a whisk
2. Add pork belly into a bag and pour contents into the bag
3. Vacuum seal it
4. Sous vide @170°f for 6-8 hours
5. Remove from bag and discard the liquids
6. Cool a bit and slice thinly to serve
