## Timing

- **Prep Time:** 10 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 15 minutes
- **Total Time:** 25 minutes
- **Servings:** 6

## Ingredients

- 12 oz. linguine pasta
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves (minced)
- 1 yellow onion (diced)
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 4 oz. goat cheese (crumbled)
- ½ cup parmesan (grated) + more for garnish
- 12 oz. jar roasted red peppers (drained and chopped)
- kosher salt
- black pepper (freshly ground)

## Instructions

1. In a large stock pot, boil salted water
2. Cook the pasta, following package instructions. You want to time the pasta to finish with the sauce.
3. Heat olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat
4. Add the onion, garlic, and a bit of salt, and cook, stirring frequently until the onions have turned translucent, about 3-5 minutes.
5. Stir in milk, heavy cream, goat cheese, and parmesan until the cheeses have melted
6. Add in the red peppers and puree with an immersion blender
7. Add in the pasta with a bit of pasta water and gently toss to combine
8. Season to taste with salt and pepper
9. Serve immediately and garnish with additional parmesan cheese as desired
