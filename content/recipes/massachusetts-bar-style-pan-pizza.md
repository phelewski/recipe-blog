## Timing

- **Prep Time:** 15 minutes + overnight proof
- **Cook Time:** 10 minutes
- **Total Time:** 1 hour
- **Servings:** 4 10" steel pan pizza doughs

## Ingredients

- 425g all-purpose flour
- 8 oz. 110°f water
- 7g sugar
- 7g kosher salt
- 7g active dry yeast
- 28g corn oil
- 4 Tbsp unsalted butter (melted)

## Instructions

1. Combine yeast, water, sugar, oil, and butter in a stand mixer bowl. Whisk together and let bloom for 5 minutes.
2. Add flour and salt to the bowl and mix with dough hook until everything is integrated and a dough ball is formed
3. Coat the dough ball lightly with oil and let proof in a container in the fridge overnight.
4. Remove dough from fridge 3 hours before cooking and let rest
5. Pre-heat oven to 500°f with convection
6. Divide the dough into 4 equal portions and flatten them into the bottom of a lightly oiled round 10" steel pan
7. Let them proof in the pans and flatten out slightly and stretch the dough to the edges over 30 minutes
8. Flatten and deflate the dough and press down firmly with finger tips into the pan to prevent large bubbles from forming during cooking process
9. Bake on convection @500°f directly on pizza steel
