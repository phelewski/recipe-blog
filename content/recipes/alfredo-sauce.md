## Timing

- **Prep Time:** 5 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 10 minutes
- **Total Time:** 15 minutes
- **Servings:** 8

## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 ½ cups heavy cream
- 2 tsp garlic (minced)
- ½ tsp italian seasoning
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp pepper
- 2 cups parmesan cheese (freshly grated)

## Instructions

1. Add the butter and cream to a medium saucepan and simmer on low heat for 2 minutes
2. Whisk in garlic and the seasonings for 2 more minutes
3. Reduce heat to the lowest setting and slowly whisk in the cheese in several batches, making sure it all melts smoothly before adding in more
4. Serve immediately
