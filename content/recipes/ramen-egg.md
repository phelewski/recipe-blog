## Timing

- **Prep Time:** 5 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 10 minutes
- **Total Time:** 20 minutes
- **Servings:** 2-3

## Ingredients

- 2 large eggs (cold)
- 2 Tbsp soy sauce
- 2 Tbsp mirin
- 2 Tbsp water

## Instructions

1. Combine the soy sauce, mirin, & water into a plastic bag in a bowl and set aside
2. Bring a small pot of water to a light boil (there should be enough water to cover the eggs completely)
3. Gently place the eggs into the boiling water, reduce heat to maintain a simmer, and cook for exactly 7 minutes
4. After 7 minutes, remove the eggs and soak in an ice bath for 3 minutes
5. Gently peel the soft boiled eggs and place them in the plastic bag
6. Close the bag tightly, removing as much air as possible and let them marinate in the fridge for at least 4 hours, overnight is better
7. Remove the eggs and discard the liquid marinade
8. Cut the eggs in half to serve
