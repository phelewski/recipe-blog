## Timing

- **Prep Time:** 5 minutes
- **Cook Time:** 10 minutes
- **Total Time:** 15 minutes
- **Servings:** 8

## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp butter
- 3 Tbsp all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup milk
- ⅔ cup beer
- 1 tsp worcestershire sauce
- 1 tsp dijon mustard
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ¼ tsp paprika
- ¼ tsp salt
- 8 oz. sharp cheddar cheese (grated)

## Instructions

1. Shred the cheese and set aside for later use
2. Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat
3. Add the flour and whisk together to form a roux. Whisk for 1-2 minutes to cook off the raw flour and to get a bit of color.
4. Slowly whisk in the milk, ensuring to break up all clumps before continuing
5. Slowly whisk in the beer, ensuring to break up all clumps before continuing
6. Add in the remaining ingredients, withholding the grated cheese, and whisk until it's fully incorporated
7. Reduce the heat to low and slowly whisk in the cheese in 3 batches, making sure it all melts smoothly before adding in more
8. The sauce is ready to serve
